## User Roles and Calendar Access

| Account Type | Calendar Access                       |
|--------------|-------------------------------------|
| Admin        | View all reservations in Admin Calendar |
| Regular User | View only own reservations in User Calendar |

---

## Calendar Views (Single Day)

### Pool Calendar
- 13 rows representing each hour (8:00 to 20:00) vertically on the left.
- 8 columns representing lanes: Lane 1 to Lane 8.
- Time labels displayed on the left side of rows.

### Open Water Calendar
- Two tables to manage and manually assign boats to buoys:
  - **Table 1:** Columns - Buoy, Boat, AM Reservation
  - **Table 2:** Columns - Buoy, Boat, PM Reservation
- Admin Dashboard includes a 1x8 boat capacity grid box showing boat numbers and diver counts:

| 1 | _ | 2 | _ | 3 | _ | 4 | _ |

- Numbers 1 to 4 indicate boat numbers; underscores are placeholders for diver/passenger counts.

### Classroom Calendar
- 13 rows for each hour (8:00 to 20:00) vertically on left.
- 3 columns representing classrooms: Room 1 to Room 3.
